Key Players

Jayson Tatum (#0)

Position: Small Forward

Tatum is one of the best players in the entire NBA and a cornerstone of the Celtics franchise. He is a multi-time All-Star and All-NBA player.

Jaylen Brown (#7)

Position: Shooting Guard

Brown brings elite athleticism, defensive intensity, and leadership. He was named to the All-NBA Second Team and is a key part of the team's two-way identity.

Derrick White (#9)

Position: Point Guard

A smart, unselfish player and strong defender, White is a fan favorite known for his consistency and hustle on both ends of the court.

Kristaps Porziņģis (#8)

Position: Center

Nicknamed "The Unicorn" for his unique skill set, Porziņģis combines elite shot-blocking with three-point shooting, giving Boston a new offensive weapon.

Jrue Holiday (#4)

Position: Point Guard

A championship-winning veteran, Jrue is widely considered one of the best defensive guards in the league. He brings calm leadership and playoff experience.
